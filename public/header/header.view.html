Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to Check In

    You can click on any event in the "Events" page and click "Check in".

  • How to Keep Track of My Points

    Your current point is show in the homepage, and will update with every event you've participated in.

  • How to Keep Track of My Activity Log

    You can access your personal activity log by selecting "Activity Log" from the dropdown under your username, located in the top right corner.

  • How to Find Events that I Have Preference For?

    In the events list page, there's a blue filter button besides the search bar. Click on it and make your preference in the pop-up window, then click "Search" to select your prefered events:)
